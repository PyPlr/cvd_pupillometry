Developers¶

PyPlr was developed at The University of Oxford by Dr Joel T. Martin with help and support from Dr Manuel Spitschan and Dr Daniel Bulte.

The project is currently maintained on GitHub by Joel and Manuel.

Issues, bugs and feature requests¶

Please use GitHub’s issue tracker.

Contribute¶
